How the X6 Matrix Works

📌 Entry split: every level price splits 40% → direct sponsor (treasury if the sponsor lacks the level) and 60% → matrix engine, which pays L1 20% / L2 25% / L3 30% / L4 25% of the matrix value up the placement chain.


🔷 Structure: each level is a 4-layer tree under you — 2 + 4 + 8 + 16 = 30 positions. Spillover mirrors every placement into your ancestors' deeper layers.


🔄 Funded recycle: the last 4 Layer-4 shares are held in-contract as fuel. When the 30th position fills, your matrix empties and the fuel re-places you under your upline like a fresh entry. If you haven't bought the next level, the fresh matrix is blocked and its income passes up until you upgrade.

How to Register
  1. Connect your MetaMask wallet
  2. Make sure you hold enough deposit tokens for level 1
  3. Enter your sponsor's address and click Register
  4. Confirm the approve transaction (first time only), then the registration
  5. You are placed in your sponsor's chain automatically (spillover applies)
Rank Progression
Rank = layers of your level-1 matrix filled: Layer 1 (2 users) → rank 1 · Layer 2 (4) → rank 2 · Layer 3 (8) → rank 3 · Layer 4 (16) → rank 4 (max). Once achieved, a rank never drops.
